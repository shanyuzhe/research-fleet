## Phrasing red lines

1. Public terminology only — internal codenames/run slugs never appear
   (mapping table below).
2. Design choices get their positive rationale in ≤1 sentence each; dead
   alternatives live in the appendix decision table only.
3. Negative results are boundary statements ("we delineate where X applies"),
   never apologies.
4. Caveats are contained in Limitations (fed by the disclosure checklist in
   `claims/README.md`) — they do not dilute the results narrative.
